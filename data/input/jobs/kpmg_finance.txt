Our corporate accounting department is looking for 2 undergraduate and 2 graduate students who are interested in pursuing a career in corporate Finance/Accounting. The interns will have the opportunity to experience what corporate Finance looks like by a 12-week rotation gaining knowledge in the areas of FP&A, Treasury, Audit, Tax and CAO Office.

Roles & Responsibilities
• Successfully complete a 12-week rotational program in corporate finance in the areas of FP&A, Treasury, Audit, Tax and CAO Office.
• Supports and assists the Treasury Leadership team in identifying and executing process improvement initiatives
• Support various treasury projects by capturing meeting minutes, preparing project plans, creating diagrams, flow charts, and power point presentations. Prepare analysis as needed.
• Timely responds to notices and other documentation related to bank group, credit reviews, treasury agreements, letters of credit, and wire transfers.
• Prepares documentation and follows procedures that comply with internal controls.
• Participates in and provides other support, reports and analysis as requested.
• Providing the highest level of customer service supporting Operations, Finance & Accounting functions, and banking partners, and financial providers/vendors.

Min. Qualifications
• Must be at least 18 years of age
• junior or senior undergraduate or master’s degree student
• Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without requiring sponsorship for employment visa status

Preferred Qualifications
• Working toward a bachelor’s and/or master’s degree in Business Finance or Accounting related field
• GPA of 3.5 overall or higher
• Knowledge of Microsoft Office Tools (Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
• Visio, preferred but not required
• Excellent interpersonal, customer service, and communication skills
• Skilled at managing multiple tasks and academic projects effectively, engaging in conflict resolution and interacting in cross-functional groups
• Effective at transferring technical knowledge within a small group or larger team
• Prior working experience- preferred but not required
• Python, Power BI knowledge or working experience- preferred but not required
