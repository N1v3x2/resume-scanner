Technology Internship Program - Summer 2024

Key Role Details

This is a full-time paid internship program spanning ten weeks from June through August 2024.

Participation in the internship requires that you are located in the continental United States with in-person attendance at your assigned location, in accordance with Capital One’s hybrid working model , for the duration of the program.

Interns will be evaluated for a full-time position within our rotational programs, with start dates six months to one year following the completion of the internship.

At this time, Capital One will not sponsor a new applicant for employment authorization for this position.

Basic Qualifications

Currently pursuing a Bachelors or higher with an expectation that the most recent degree will be obtained by August 2025 or earlier

Must be continuing in the same course of study of the most recent degree following completion of the internship

Preferred Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree or higher in a technical or quantitative discipline, including computer science, computer engineering, information systems, information technology, and mathematics

Strong analytical, quantitative, problem solving and conceptual skills

Strong interpersonal, leadership and communication skills

Intermediate to advanced programming skills or experience in one or more of the following areas: Java, .net, asp.net, PHP, Python, Objective C, C#, C/C++, Perl, XML, JSON, UML, JavaScript, Visual Basic, Swift, Go, Kotlin, HTML, SQL, PL/SQL, Shell scripts

As a Software Engineer in the Technology Internship Program, you may work on everything from customer-facing web and mobile applications using cutting-edge open source frameworks, to highly-available RESTful services, to back-end Java based systems using the hottest techniques in Big Data. Our engineers work in a you "You Build It, You Own It" full stack environment supporting an exciting and innovative tech stack composed of: Java, React, Angular, Vue JS, C#, REST APIs, NoSQL database, Kubernetes, Terraform, Jenkins, Docker, AWS cloud, Scala, Clojure, Java, Haskell, C++, golang, Scheme, Common Lisp, and more! The Technology Internship Program provides you the framework to develop world-class technical skills and gain a comprehensive understanding of technology.

Here is what to expect during your 10 week internship:

A challenging role where you’ll collaborate with a diverse and passionate team of talented engineers

Engaging executive speakers and opportunities to interact closely with senior leadership

Training & development sessions to build out your skillset on topics such iOS and Android, Web Development, AWS, Open Source Big Data tools, Security, Voice Driven Applications, and more

Fun social, networking, and volunteer events with other college interns
